# Finance & Bureaucracy Resources

Curated collection of free, high-value resources to improve financial literacy, investing, personal finance, economic understanding, and navigating bureaucratic systems.

---

## 1. Free Guides, PDFs & Manuals

- SEC Investor Education: Beginners Guide to Investing – https://www.investor.gov/introduction-investing  
- FINRA Investor Education – https://www.finra.org/investors  
- Federal Reserve Education: Economics & Personal Finance – https://www.federalreserveeducation.org  
- Personal Finance for Beginners (Open Textbook) – https://openstax.org/details/books/personal-finance  
- OECD Government at a Glance – Free PDF reports on governance & bureaucracy – https://www.oecd.org/gov/government-at-a-glance.htm  
- USA.gov: How Government Works (Citizen Guide) – https://www.usa.gov/branches-of-government
