# Volunteering Resources

A curated collection of websites and platforms to find verified volunteering opportunities across multiple domains. Focused on genuine impact and accessibility.

---

## 1. Global Volunteering Platforms

- **VolunteerMatch** – https://www.volunteermatch.org : Connects volunteers with nonprofits worldwide.  
- **Idealist** – https://www.idealist.org : Global nonprofit and NGO volunteering opportunities.  
- **UN Volunteers** – https://www.onlinevolunteering.org : Volunteer for UN projects remotely or in-person.  
- **All for Good** – https://www.allforgood.org : Platform aggregating volunteer opportunities globally.  
- **Catchafire** – https://www.catchafire.org : Skill-based volunteering for nonprofits.

## 6. Student & Community Volunteering

- **AIESEC** – https://aiesec.org/volunteer : Youth volunteering and leadership projects worldwide.  
- **Local Meetup or Facebook Groups** – Search for community volunteering events in your city.  
- **Rotaract & Rotary International** – https://www.rotary.org/en/get-involved : Community service projects globally.  
- **DoSomething.org** – https://www.dosomething.org : Campaign-based volunteering for youth.  
- **GlobalGiving** – https://www.globalgiving.org : Volunteer and support projects worldwide.  

---

> ⚠️ **Tips for Safe Volunteering:**  
> - Verify legitimacy before providing personal information.  
> - Prefer established organizations with transparent programs.  
> - Start with virtual volunteering if unsure about in-person safety.  
> - Focus on causes that align with your skills and interests.
